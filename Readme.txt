This program is made to run on ESP8266, developed on the Espressif's type. There are several peripheral hardware required for the hardware to run properly, which are:

> 12 Volt solenoid valve x2
> Switch Relay Unit, 12V in 5V out
> YFS-201 water flow center
> DHT 22
> Soil Moisture sensor
> Container for liquid fertilizer
> Water source (may be container, or straight from water pipeline)

Other requirements are a ThingSpeak Account with 2 channels setup, and a stable accesspoint.
